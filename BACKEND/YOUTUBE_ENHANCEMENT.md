## 🧠 Smart Ranking Algorithm

### **Scoring System:**
- **+50-150 points**: Top medical channels (higher tier = more points)
- **+2 points**: Medical keywords (treatment, symptoms, diagnosis, etc.)
- **+3 points**: Educational indicators (explained, guide, how to)
- **-5 points**: Spam/clickbait keywords (shocking, secret, miracle)

### **Search Strategy:**
1. **First attempt**: Direct search for condition + treatment
2. **Second attempt**: Search with specific top medical channels
3. **Third attempt**: Enhanced medical terminology search
4. **Result**: Returns highest-scoring video from best available channel
