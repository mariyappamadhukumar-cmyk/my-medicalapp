🚀 How to Use

1

Look for the Hamburger Button

In the top-left corner of your dashboard, you'll see a blue/purple button with three horizontal lines (≡).

2

Click to Open

Click the button and watch the sidebar slide in from the left. The button transforms into an X icon.

3

Browse Menu Options

See all 12 options organized into Main Menu (4 items), Health Services (4 items), and Settings (4 items).

4

Click Any Option

Select any menu item to navigate. Dashboard is already highlighted as the active page.

5

Close the Menu

Click the X button, click the dark overlay, or click outside the sidebar to close it.
