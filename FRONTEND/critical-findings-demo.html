🚨 Critical Findings Feature

Track the most concerning health conditions detected in your analyses

✨ What's New

Your dashboard now displays the most critical diseases found in each time period!

When you select "This Week", "This Month", "This Year", or "All Time", the dashboard will show:
  • All detected conditions from your cough analyses
  • Severity level (Critical, Moderate, Low)
  • How many times each condition was detected
  • Pattern and frequency details
  • When it was last detected

🎯 Key Features

⚠️
Severity-Based Sorting
Critical conditions appear first, followed by moderate and low severity conditions.
🔢
Detection Counter
See how many times each condition has been detected in the selected time period.
🎨
Color-Coded Cards
Red for critical, orange for moderate, green for low severity conditions.
📅
Time Filtering
Filter by week, month, year, or all time to track condition trends.
📊
Detailed Metrics
View pattern type, dominant frequency, and exact detection dates.
🔄
Auto-Updates
Updates automatically when you add new cough analyses or change time filters.

🚀 How to Use

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Open Health Dashboard - Go to health-dashboard.html
  2. Select Time Period - Click "This Week", "This Month", "This Year", or "All Time"
  3. View Critical Findings - Scroll down to see the "⚠️ Critical Findings" section
  4. Review Conditions - Check severity, patterns, and detection counts
  5. Track Changes - Do more analyses to see how conditions evolve
